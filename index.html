Our Story

A Decade of Voice Crafting

Angel Coaching Classes began with a simple observation: brilliant minds were being held back not by their ideas, but by how they expressed them. In 2000, "Harsha Rajput" saw that English fluency wasn't just about grammar—it was about confidence, presence, and the subtle art of being heard.

What started as intimate coaching sessions has grown into a refined methodology that has transformed over 1,000+ voices across 2+ cities. We've learned that true fluency isn't about sounding native—it's about sounding authentic, clear, and compelling.
